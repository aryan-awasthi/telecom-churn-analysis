# 📊 Telecom Churn Analysis

## 🔹 Objective  
Analyze customer churn behavior and identify key factors influencing customer attrition in a telecom dataset.

---

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies  
- SQL (MySQL Workbench)  
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) 
- Tableau  
- Excel  

---

## 🔹 Dataset  
Telecom customer dataset containing:
- Customer demographics  
- Contract type  
- Internet service  
- Payment method  
- Monthly charges  
- Churn status  


---

## 🔹 Key Analysis  

### 1. Overall KPI  
- Total customers  
- Churned customers  
- Churn rate  

### 2. Churn by Contract  
- Month-to-month → highest churn  
- Long-term → lower churn  

### 3. Churn by Internet Type  
- Fiber optic users show higher churn than DSL  

### 4. Churn by Payment Method  
- Bank withdrawal & similar methods show higher churn  

### 5. Churn by Pricing Segment  
- Medium & low pricing segments have higher churn  

---

## 🔹 Key Insights  

- 📉 Month-to-month contracts drive churn  
- 🌐 Fiber users are high-risk customers  
- 💳 Payment behavior impacts retention  
- 💰 Pricing segmentation reveals churn patterns  

---

## 🔹 Business Impact  

- Encourage long-term contracts  
- Improve fiber service experience  
- Target high-risk customers  
- Optimize pricing strategy
