## 🔹 Key Insights  

### 1. Overall KPI  
- Total customers  
- Churned customers  
- Churn rate  

### 2. Churn by Contract  
- Month-to-month → highest churn  
- Long-term → lower churn  

### 3. Churn by Internet Type  
- Fiber optic users show higher churn than DSL  

### 4. Churn by Payment Method  
- Bank withdrawal & similar methods show higher churn  

### 5. Churn by Pricing Segment  
- Medium & low pricing segments have higher churn  

---



## 🔹 Business Impact  

- Encourage long-term contracts  
- Improve fiber service experience  
- Target high-risk customers  
- Optimize pricing strategy
